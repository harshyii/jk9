<h2>Safety Tips While Using an Angle Grinder</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow these safety practices before operating an angle grinder.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear certified safety goggles or a face shield.</li>

<li>Use gloves suitable for metalworking.</li>

<li>Always fit the wheel guard.</li>

<li>Inspect cutting discs before use.</li>

<li>Never exceed the disc's rated RPM.</li>

<li>Allow the grinder to reach full speed before cutting.</li>

<li>Secure the workpiece firmly.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Keep both hands on the grinder during operation.</li>

</ul>

</div>
