<h2>Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow these safety practices while operating a drill machine.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear safety glasses before drilling.</li>

<li>Secure the workpiece firmly.</li>

<li>Select the correct drill bit for the material.</li>

<li>Do not force the machine while drilling.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing drill bits.</li>

<li>Keep hands away from rotating components.</li>

<li>Inspect cables, batteries and chuck regularly.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged operation.</li>

</ul>

</div>
