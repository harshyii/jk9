<h2 class="mt-5 mb-4">
How to Maintain Hand Tools
</h2>

<p>

Even the best hand tools require proper care. Regular maintenance extends tool life, improves safety, and ensures reliable performance for years.

</p>

<div class="accordion mb-5" id="maintenanceAccordion">

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button
class="accordion-button"
data-bs-toggle="collapse"
data-bs-target="#m1">

Clean After Every Use

</button>

</h2>

<div
id="m1"
class="accordion-collapse collapse show"
data-bs-parent="#maintenanceAccordion">

<div class="accordion-body">

Remove dust, grease, cement, wood particles, and metal filings using a clean cloth or soft brush before storing tools.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button
class="accordion-button collapsed"
data-bs-toggle="collapse"
data-bs-target="#m2">

Prevent Rust

</button>

</h2>

<div
id="m2"
class="accordion-collapse collapse"
data-bs-parent="#maintenanceAccordion">

<div class="accordion-body">

Apply a thin layer of machine oil to steel surfaces and store tools in a dry toolbox with silica gel packs to reduce moisture.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button
class="accordion-button collapsed"
data-bs-toggle="collapse"
data-bs-target="#m3">

Inspect Regularly

</button>

</h2>

<div
id="m3"
class="accordion-collapse collapse"
data-bs-parent="#maintenanceAccordion">

<div class="accordion-body">

Replace cracked handles, damaged jaws, loose screws, and worn cutting edges immediately to avoid accidents.

</div>

</div>

</div>
