## Important Features to Consider Before Buying

<div class="accordion my-4" id="features">

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#motor">

Motor Power

</button>

</h2>

<div id="motor" class="accordion-collapse collapse show">

<div class="accordion-body">

For regular woodworking, choose a jigsaw with <strong>500–800 Watts</strong>. More power provides smoother cutting in hardwood and thicker timber.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#speed">

Variable Speed Control

</button>

</h2>

<div id="speed" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Variable speed allows slower cuts in plastic and faster cuts in plywood and timber. This improves blade life and cutting quality.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#stroke">

Stroke Length

</button>

</h2>

<div id="stroke" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Longer stroke lengths remove more material per stroke, making cutting faster without sacrificing accuracy.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#orbital">

Orbital Action

</button>

</h2>

<div id="orbital" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Orbital movement increases cutting speed in wood by moving the blade slightly forward during the cutting stroke.

</div>

</div>

</div>

</div>

## Common Mistakes When Buying a Jigsaw

Many buyers focus only on price, but selecting the wrong machine can lead to poor cutting quality, faster blade wear, and unnecessary frustration.

<div class="row g-4 my-4">

<div class="col-md-6">

<div class="card border-danger shadow-sm h-100">

<div class="card-body">

<h5>❌ Buying Only by Price</h5>

Low-cost machines often have weaker motors, excessive vibration, and lower cutting accuracy.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-md-6">

<div class="card border-danger shadow-sm h-100">

<div class="card-body">

<h5>❌ Ignoring Blade Quality</h5>

Even the best jigsaw performs poorly with a low-quality blade.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-md-6">

<div class="card border-danger shadow-sm h-100">

<div class="card-body">

<h5>❌ Using the Wrong Blade</h5>

Different materials require different blade tooth designs for clean and safe cuts.

</div>

</div>

<div class="card border-danger shadow-sm h-100">

## Essential Safety Tips

<div class="alert alert-warning shadow-sm">

Always follow these safety practices before operating a jigsaw.

</div>

<ul class="list-group mb-5">

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Wear safety goggles to protect your eyes.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Secure the workpiece with clamps.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Keep both hands away from the cutting line.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Inspect the blade before every use.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Allow the blade to reach full speed before cutting.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Disconnect power before changing blades.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Never force the machine through thick material.</li>

<li class="list-group-item">✔ Use dust extraction whenever possible.</li>

</ul>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<div class="accordion mb-5" id="faq">

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#q1">

Can a jigsaw cut thick wood?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="q1" class="accordion-collapse collapse show">

<div class="accordion-body">

Yes. Most professional jigsaws can cut wood up to 80–150 mm depending on motor power and blade quality.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#q2">

Can a jigsaw cut metal?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="q2" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Yes, provided you use a metal-cutting blade and reduce the cutting speed.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#q3">

Is a cordless jigsaw better?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="q3" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Cordless models provide excellent portability, while corded machines deliver continuous power for long workshop sessions.

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#q4">

How long does a jigsaw blade last?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="q4" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Blade life depends on the material being cut, blade quality, and operating technique. Replace blades as soon as they become dull.
