<h2>Why Install an On-Grid Solar System?</h2>

<p>An on-grid solar system is directly connected to the electricity grid through a solar inverter. During the day, electricity generated by the solar panels powers your building first. Any excess electricity is exported to the grid through net metering, helping reduce your monthly electricity bill.</p>

<h2>How Does Net Metering Work?</h2>

<p>Net metering allows excess electricity generated by your solar panels to be exported to the electricity grid. When your solar system produces more power than your building consumes, the additional electricity is sent back to the grid, reducing future electricity bills.</p>

<div class="row g-3">

<div class="col-md-6">

<div class="card h-100">

<div class="card-body">

<h5>Daytime</h5>

<p class="mb-0">

</p>

</div>

</div>

</div>

<div class="col-md-6">

<div class="card h-100">

<div class="card-body">

<h5>Night Time</h5>

<p class="mb-0">

Electricity is imported from the grid as usual. Net metering adjusts the final electricity bill accordingly.
