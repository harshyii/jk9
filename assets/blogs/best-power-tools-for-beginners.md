<h2>Common Buying Mistakes</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Avoid these mistakes when purchasing your first power tools.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Buying the cheapest machine available.</li>

<li>Ignoring warranty and service support.</li>

<li>Choosing oversized tools for small jobs.</li>

<li>Using poor quality accessories.</li>
