<h1>10 Common Angle Grinder Mistakes That Can Damage Your Tool or Cause Injury</h1>

<p>Angle grinders are among the most useful power tools in any [workshop](blog.html?id=workshop-safety-checklist), but they're also one of the most dangerous when used incorrectly. Many accidents happen because of small mistakes that are easy to avoid. This guide explains the most common errors and how to use your grinder safely and efficiently.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Why [Safety](blog.html?id=warehouse-safety-guide) Matters</h2>

<div class="alert alert-warning">
Even experienced professionals can damage tools or suffer serious injuries by ignoring basic operating practices. Always inspect both the grinder and the cutting disc before starting work.
</div>

<hr>

<h2>Top 10 Mistakes</h2>

<div class="accordion" id="mistakes">

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m1">1. Using the Wrong Disc</button></h2>
<div id="m1" class="accordion-collapse collapse show">
<div class="accordion-body">
Never use a grinding wheel for cutting or a cutting wheel for grinding. Always match the accessory to the intended application.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m2">2. Removing the Safety Guard</button></h2>
<div id="m2" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
The guard protects you from sparks, broken discs and kickback. Removing it greatly increases the risk of injury.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m3">3. Applying Too Much Pressure</button></h2>
<div id="m3" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Let the disc do the work. Excessive force overheats the motor, wears discs quickly and reduces cutting efficiency.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m4">4. Using Damaged Discs</button></h2>
<div id="m4" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Never use cracked, chipped or expired abrasive discs. Replace damaged accessories immediately.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m5">5. Ignoring RPM Ratings</button></h2>
<div id="m5" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
The disc's maximum RPM must always be equal to or higher than the grinder's speed.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m6">6. Not Wearing PPE</button></h2>
<div id="m6" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Always wear safety glasses, gloves, hearing protection and suitable footwear before operating an angle grinder.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m7">7. Holding the Tool with One Hand</button></h2>
<div id="m7" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Always use the auxiliary side handle for better control and to reduce the risk of kickback.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m8">8. Cutting Unsecured Material</button></h2>
<div id="m8" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Clamp the workpiece securely before cutting. Moving material can pinch the disc and cause kickback.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m9">9. Skipping Maintenance</button></h2>
<div id="m9" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Regularly clean ventilation slots, inspect carbon brushes and check the power cord for damage.
</div>
</div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#m10">10. Using the Wrong Disc Size</button></h2>
<div id="m10" class="accordion-collapse collapse">
<div class="accordion-body">
Never install oversized discs. Always use the disc diameter recommended by the manufacturer.
</div>
</div>
</div>

</div>

<h2>Frequently Asked Questions</h2>

<div class="accordion" id="faq">

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq1">Can I remove the safety guard?</button></h2>
<div id="faq1" class="accordion-collapse collapse show"><div class="accordion-body">No. The safety guard is an essential protective component and should always remain installed.</div></div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq2">How often should I replace grinding discs?</button></h2>
<div id="faq2" class="accordion-collapse collapse"><div class="accordion-body">Replace discs whenever they become worn, cracked, chipped or reach their expiry date.</div></div>
</div>

<div class="accordion-item">
<h2 class="accordion-header"><button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq3">Why does my grinder overheat?</button></h2>
<div id="faq3" class="accordion-collapse collapse"><div class="accordion-body">Common causes include excessive pressure, blocked air vents, worn carbon brushes or continuous heavy-duty use.</div></div>
</div>
