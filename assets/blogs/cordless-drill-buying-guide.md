## Why a Cordless Drill?

Whether you're assembling furniture, installing shelves, drilling concrete, or working on construction projects, a cordless drill is one of the most useful power tools you can own.

Modern cordless drills are:

- 🔋 Battery Powered
- ⚡ Portable
- 🔩 Powerful
- 🪵 Suitable for Wood
- 🧱 Suitable for Masonry
- 🔧 Suitable for Metal
- 🏠 Perfect for DIY
- 🏗️ Ideal for Professionals

<div class="alert alert-primary mt-4">

<strong>Quick Tip</strong>

If you're buying only one power tool, make it a cordless drill. It can replace several manual tools while saving hours of work.

</div>

## 3. Brushless vs Brushed Motors

<div class="table-responsive">

| Feature | Brushed | Brushless |
|----------|----------|-----------|
| Price | Lower | Higher |
| Efficiency | Good | Excellent |
| Heat | More | Less |
| Maintenance | More | Almost None |
| Battery Life | Average | Excellent |
| Power | Good | Excellent |

</div>

<div class="alert alert-success">

<strong>Recommendation</strong>

If your budget allows, always choose a brushless cordless drill. It lasts longer, runs cooler, and provides more torque.

</div>

## Maintenance Tips

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#tip1">

<div class="accordion-body">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#tip2">
