<h2>What are Cordless Power Tools?</h2>

<p>
Cordless tools operate using rechargeable lithium-ion batteries instead of a power cable. Modern battery technology delivers excellent performance while allowing complete freedom of movement.
</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning py-2">
<strong>Popular Cordless Tools</strong>
<ul class="mb-0 mt-2">
<li>Cordless Drill</li>
<li>Impact Driver</li>
<li>Angle Grinder</li>
<li>Circular Saw</li>
<li>Reciprocating Saw</li>
<li>Heat Gun</li>
<li>LED Work Light</li>
</ul>
</div>

<hr>

<h2>What are Corded Power Tools?</h2>

<p>
Corded tools draw electricity directly from a power outlet, providing constant power without worrying about battery charging. They remain the preferred choice for heavy-duty industrial work and continuous operation.
</p>

<div class="alert alert-success py-2">
<strong>Common Corded Tools</strong>
