<h1>Extension Cord Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Extension Board & Cable</h1>

<p>An extension cord is one of the most commonly used electrical accessories in homes, offices, workshops and industrial sites. Choosing the wrong extension cord can lead to voltage drop, overheating, damaged equipment and even fire hazards. This guide explains everything you should know before purchasing an extension cord.</p>

<hr>

<h2>What is an Extension Cord?</h2>

<p>An extension cord is a flexible electrical cable that allows power to reach equipment located away from a wall socket. It consists of a plug, insulated cable and one or more output sockets.</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning py-2">
<strong>Common Applications</strong>
<ul class="mb-0 mt-2">
<li>Power tools</li>
<li>Home appliances</li>
<li>Garden equipment</li>
<li>Construction sites</li>
<li>Office electronics</li>
<li>Industrial machinery</li>
</ul>
</div>

<h2>Avoid These Common Mistakes</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">
<ul class="mb-0">
<li>Do not overload the extension cord.</li>
<li>Never connect one extension board into another.</li>
<li>Avoid running cables under carpets.</li>
<li>Replace damaged plugs immediately.</li>
<li>Never use indoor extension cords outdoors.</li>
<li>Do not leave heavy appliances connected continuously.</li>
</ul>
</div>
