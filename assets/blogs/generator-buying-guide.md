<h1>Generator Buying Guide: How to Choose the Right Generator for Home, Business & Industrial Use</h1>

<p>A generator provides backup electricity during power failures and is essential for homes, shops, offices, hospitals, factories and construction sites. Choosing the right generator depends on your power requirement, fuel type, efficiency and intended application. This guide explains everything you need before purchasing a generator.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Why Do You Need a Generator?</h2>

<p>Power interruptions can stop business operations, damage sensitive equipment and create safety concerns. A properly sized generator ensures uninterrupted electricity whenever the main supply fails.</p>

<h2>Choosing the Correct Generator Size</h2>

<p>The most important factor is calculating your total electrical load. Buying an undersized generator causes overloads, while an oversized generator increases fuel consumption and purchase cost.</p>

<div class="table-responsive">

<table class="table table-striped">

<thead>
<tr>
<th>Application</th>
<th>Recommended Capacity</th>
</tr>
</thead>

<tbody>

<tr>
<td>Home Backup</td>
<td>2–5 kVA</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Large House</td>
<td>5–10 kVA</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Small Office</td>
<td>10–25 kVA</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Commercial Building</td>
<td>25–125 kVA</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Factory</td>
<td>125 kVA & Above</td>
</tr>

</tbody>

</table>

</div>

<div class="alert alert-info py-2">
<strong>Tip:</strong> Always keep 20–25% extra capacity above your estimated electrical load to handle starting current and future expansion.
</div>

<h2>Buying Checklist</h2>

<div class="alert alert-success">

Before purchasing a generator, make sure it offers:

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Reliable engine brand</li>
<li>Low fuel consumption</li>
<li>Automatic Voltage Regulation (AVR)</li>
<li>Electric start</li>
<li>Low oil shutdown</li>
<li>Easy maintenance</li>
<li>Warranty and service support</li>

<h2>Maintenance Tips</h2>

<div class="list-group mb-3">

<div class="list-group-item">✔ Change engine oil as recommended.</div>
<div class="list-group-item">✔ Clean or replace the air filter regularly.</div>
<div class="list-group-item">✔ Inspect fuel lines for leakage.</div>
<div class="list-group-item">✔ Run the generator periodically even during off-season.</div>
<div class="list-group-item">✔ Check battery condition for electric start models.</div>
<div class="list-group-item">✔ Store fuel safely and keep the generator dry.</div>
