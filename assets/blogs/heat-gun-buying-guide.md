<h1>Heat Gun Buying Guide: How to Choose the Best Heat Gun</h1>

<p>A heat gun is one of the most useful tools for workshops, electricians, automotive technicians, plumbers and DIY enthusiasts. It produces a controlled stream of hot air that can remove paint, shrink tubing, soften adhesives, bend plastic and perform countless industrial applications. This guide explains everything you should consider before buying a heat gun.</p>

<hr>

<h2>What is a Heat Gun?</h2>

<p>A heat gun is an electric tool that blows heated air at temperatures ranging from around 50°C to over 650°C. Different temperature settings make it suitable for delicate household work as well as demanding industrial applications.</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning py-2">
<strong>Common Uses</strong>
<ul class="mb-0 mt-2">
<li>Paint removal</li>
<li>Heat shrink tubing</li>
<li>PVC pipe bending</li>
<li>Sticker & adhesive removal</li>
<li>Plastic welding</li>
<li>Wire insulation work</li>
<li>Vehicle wrapping</li>
<li>Electronics repair</li>
</ul>
</div>
