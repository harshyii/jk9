<h2>What is an On-Grid Solar System?</h2>

<p>An <strong>on-grid solar system</strong>, also known as a grid-connected solar system, generates electricity using photovoltaic (PV) solar panels and directly connects to your local electricity distribution network.</p>

<p>During daylight hours, the solar panels generate electricity that powers your home or business. When your solar system produces more electricity than you consume, the excess energy is exported back to the electricity grid through a <strong>net meter</strong>. At night or during cloudy weather, electricity is automatically drawn from the grid.</p>

<h2>Net Metering in Haryana</h2>

<p>Net metering allows your electricity meter to record both the electricity you consume from the grid and the surplus electricity generated by your rooftop solar system. Excess power produced during sunny hours can be exported to the electricity grid, helping reduce your electricity bill.</p>
