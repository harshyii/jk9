<h2>Power Tool Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always operate power tools according to the manufacturer's instructions.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear approved safety glasses.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged operation.</li>

<li>Inspect power cables and batteries before use.</li>

<li>Use only recommended accessories.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing blades or discs.</li>

<li>Keep the work area clean and well-lit.</li>

<li>Never remove safety guards.</li>

<li>Store tools in a dry location after use.</li>

</ul>

</div>
