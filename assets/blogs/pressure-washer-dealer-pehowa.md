<h2>Pressure Washer Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

<strong>Always follow these safety recommendations:</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Never point the spray gun at people or animals.</li>

<li>Wear safety glasses while operating.</li>

<li>Keep electrical connections dry.</li>

<li>Use the correct nozzle for each application.</li>

<li>Switch off the machine before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Release pressure before disconnecting hoses.</li>
