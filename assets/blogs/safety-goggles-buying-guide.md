<h1>Safety Goggles [Buying Guide](blog.html?id=how-to-choose-angle-grinder): How to Choose the Right Eye Protection</h1>

<p>Whether you're cutting metal, grinding concrete, woodworking or working with chemicals, your eyes are constantly exposed to flying particles, sparks and dust. A quality pair of safety goggles is one of the most important pieces of personal protective equipment (PPE) and should never be overlooked.</p>

<hr>

<h2>Why Safety Goggles Matter</h2>

<p>Thousands of workplace eye injuries occur every year because workers either wear no protection or use the wrong type of eyewear. Proper goggles help prevent permanent eye damage caused by impact, dust, chemicals and harmful UV rays.</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning py-2">
<strong>Common Hazards</strong>
<ul class="mb-0 mt-2">
<li>Flying metal chips</li>
<li>Concrete dust</li>
<li>Grinding sparks</li>
<li>Chemical splashes</li>
<li>Wood particles</li>
<li>Glass fragments</li>
<li>UV exposure</li>
</ul>
</div>

<h2>Buying Checklist</h2>

<div class="alert alert-success">

Before buying safety goggles, ensure they provide:

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">
<li>ANSI or IS certified protection</li>
<li>Impact resistant lenses</li>

<li>Comfortable head strap</li>

<li>Full side protection</li>

Are clear lenses better than tinted lenses?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="faq2" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Clear lenses are ideal for indoor work, while smoke or tinted lenses are better suited for outdoor environments with bright sunlight.

</div>

</div>

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<button class="accordion-button collapsed" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#faq3">

How often should safety goggles be replaced?

</button>

</h2>

<div id="faq3" class="accordion-collapse collapse">

<div class="accordion-body">

Replace goggles whenever lenses become scratched, cracked, loose or visibility is reduced. Damaged eye protection should never be used.
