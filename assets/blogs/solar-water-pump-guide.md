<h2 class="mt-5">
<i class="bi bi-droplet-half text-primary me-2"></i>
What is a Solar Water Pump?
</h2>

<p>
A solar water pump is a pumping system powered entirely by electricity generated from solar photovoltaic (PV) panels. Instead of depending on grid electricity or diesel generators, solar panels convert sunlight into electrical energy that operates the pump motor.
</p>

<p>
These systems are widely used across India for:
</p>

<h2 class="mt-5">
Types of Solar Water Pumps
</h2>

<div class="accordion mb-4" id="pumpTypes">

<div class="accordion-item">

<h2 class="accordion-header">

<div id="submersible" class="accordion-collapse collapse" data-bs-parent="#pumpTypes">

<div class="accordion-body">

<h2 class="mt-5">
<i class="bi bi-calculator-fill text-success me-2"></i>
How to Choose the Right Solar Water Pump Size
</h2>

<p>
Selecting the correct pump capacity is the most important decision before purchasing a solar water pumping system. An oversized pump increases installation cost, while an undersized pump cannot meet daily water requirements.
</p>

</tr>

</thead>

<tbody>

<tr>

<td>0.5 HP</td>

<td>Home, Garden, Small Water Tank</td>

<td>500–700 W</td>

</tr>

<tr>

<td>1 HP</td>

<td>Small Farms</td>

<td>900–1200 W</td>

</tr>

<tr>

<td>Medium Farms</td>

<td>1800–2400 W</td>

</tr>

<tr>

</tr>

<tr>

<td>4500–6000 W</td>

<h2 class="mt-5">
<i class="bi bi-battery-charging text-warning me-2"></i>
Do Solar Water Pumps Need Batteries?
</h2>

<p>
Most agricultural solar water pumping systems do <strong>not</strong> use batteries. The pump runs directly from solar panels during daylight hours. Instead of storing electricity in batteries, excess water is stored in an overhead tank for use later.
</p>

<div class="alert alert-success">

<h5 class="mb-3">
Advantages of Battery-Free Systems
</h5>

<ul class="mb-0">

<li>Lower installation cost</li>

<li>No battery replacement expense</li>

<li>Minimal maintenance</li>

<li>Higher overall efficiency</li>

<li>Longer system life</li>

</ul>

</div>
