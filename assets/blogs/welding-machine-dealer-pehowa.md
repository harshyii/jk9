<h2>Welding Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow safe welding practices.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear an approved welding helmet with the correct shade.</li>

<li>Use heat-resistant welding gloves.</li>

<li>Wear flame-resistant clothing.</li>

<li>Ensure proper workplace ventilation.</li>

<li>Inspect welding cables before use.</li>

<li>Keep flammable materials away from the work area.</li>

<li>Never weld on sealed containers.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before servicing the machine.</li>

</ul>

</div>
