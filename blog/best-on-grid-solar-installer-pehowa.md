## What is an On-Grid Solar System?

An on-grid solar system is connected directly to the electricity grid. During the daytime, your solar panels generate electricity that powers your property. Any surplus energy can be exported to the grid through net metering, helping reduce your electricity bills significantly.

### Benefits

- Lower monthly electricity bills
- Government-approved net metering
- Low maintenance
- Long panel life
- Environment-friendly energy
- Excellent return on investment
