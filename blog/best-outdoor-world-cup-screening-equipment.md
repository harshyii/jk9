<h2>Cable Management & Safety</h2>

<p>

Loose electrical cables are one of the biggest hazards during outdoor events. Proper cable routing improves safety while keeping the venue neat and professional.

</p>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

<strong>Safety Tips</strong>

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Keep power cables away from walkways.</li>

<li>Cover exposed cables using cable protectors.</li>

<li>Use weatherproof extension boards outdoors.</li>

<li>Avoid overloading a single extension board.</li>

<li>Inspect all plugs before connecting equipment.</li>

<li>Keep electrical joints away from rain and moisture.</li>

<li>Never run damaged extension cables.</li>

<li>Disconnect equipment safely after the event.</li>

</ul>

</div>
