<h2>Essential Safety Tips</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Always follow these safety practices before operating any power tool.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Wear approved eye protection.</li>

<li>Use hearing protection during prolonged operation.</li>

<li>Read the manufacturer's operating manual.</li>

<li>Inspect cables, batteries and accessories before use.</li>

<li>Use the correct blade, disc or bit for the application.</li>

<li>Keep both hands on the tool whenever possible.</li>

<li>Disconnect power before changing accessories.</li>

<li>Keep the work area clean and well illuminated.</li>

</ul>
