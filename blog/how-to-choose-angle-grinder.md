<h1>How to Choose the Right Angle Grinder for Industrial and Home Use</h1>

<p>
An angle grinder is one of the most versatile power tools available. Whether you're cutting steel, grinding welds, polishing metal or removing rust, choosing the right grinder can improve productivity, safety and finishing quality. This guide explains the important factors you should consider before buying an angle grinder.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>What is an Angle Grinder?</h2>

<p>
An angle grinder is a handheld power tool that rotates abrasive discs at high speed. Depending on the accessory used, it can cut, grind, sand, polish, sharpen and clean a wide range of materials including metal, stone, concrete and tiles.
</p>

<div class="alert alert-warning py-2">
<strong>Common Applications</strong>

<ul class="mb-0 mt-2">
<li>Metal cutting</li>
<li>Weld grinding</li>
<li>Rust removal</li>
<li>Tile and marble cutting</li>
<li>Surface polishing</li>
<li>Concrete grinding</li>
</ul>
</div>
