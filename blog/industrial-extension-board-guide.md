<h1>Industrial Extension Board Buying Guide: How to Choose a Safe Power Strip</h1>

<p>An extension board is more than just extra sockets. In homes, workshops, factories and construction sites, choosing the wrong extension board can cause overheating, equipment damage and even electrical fires. This guide explains everything you should check before purchasing an industrial extension board.</p>

<h2>Common Buying Mistakes</h2>

<div class="alert alert-danger">

Avoid these common mistakes while purchasing an extension board.

<ul class="mt-2 mb-0">

<li>Buying only based on the number of sockets.</li>

<li>Ignoring the current rating.</li>

<li>Using aluminium wiring instead of copper.</li>

<li>Connecting multiple heavy machines to one board.</li>

<li>Using damaged or loose extension cords.</li>

<li>Using indoor extension boards outdoors.</li>

<li>Ignoring earthing protection.</li>

</ul>

</div>

<hr>

<div class="alert alert-success">
